## Prioritisation Frameworks

| Framework | What It Is (one sentence) | Who Uses It | Source | Related Blueprint |
|-----------|--------------------------|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| RICE | Reach x Impact x Confidence / Effort scoring for ranking features and projects | Intercom, widespread | `preparing-for-a-pm-interview.md`, `matt-lemay.md` | Blueprint - Product Vision and Roadmapping |
| ICE Scoring | Impact x Confidence x Ease -- a lighter-weight cousin of RICE | Widespread | `matt-lemay.md` | Blueprint - Product Vision and Roadmapping |
| Eisenhower Matrix | 2x2 of Urgent vs Important to triage tasks into Do / Schedule / Delegate / Delete quadrants | Stephen Covey / widespread | `my-favorite-decision-making-frameworks.md` | Blueprint - Product Delivery |
| LNO Framework | Classify every task as Leverage, Neutral, or Overhead to focus time on high-leverage work | Shreyas Doshi | `time-management-techniques-that-actually-work.md` | Blueprint - Strategic Impact |
| Impact-Effort Matrix | Simple 2x2 plotting impact against effort to surface quick wins and deprioritise low-value work | Widespread | `the-definitive-guide-to-mastering-product-sense-interviews.md`, `camille-hearst.md` | Blueprint - Product Vision and Roadmapping |
| GEM Framework | Grade ideas on Growth, Engagement, and Monetisation to prioritise the product roadmap | Gibson Biddle / Netflix | `gibson-biddle.md` | Blueprint - Product Vision and Roadmapping |
| Stack Ranking | Force-rank every initiative against a single criterion to eliminate "everything is P0" thinking | Various (Coda, Airbnb) | `how-coda-builds-product.md` | Blueprint - Product Vision and Roadmapping |
| Cost of Delay | Quantify the economic cost of not shipping a feature per unit of time to prioritise by urgency and value | Widespread (SAFe origins) | `the-definitive-guide-to-mastering-product-sense-interviews.md` | Blueprint - Product Delivery |
| $100 Voting Pulse | Each participant distributes 100 virtual dollars across options to surface true group priorities | Shishir Mehrotra / Coda | `my-favorite-decision-making-frameworks.md` | Blueprint - Product Operations and Scaling Orgs |
