## Retention Benchmarks (User Retention at 6 Months)

| Metric | Good | Great | Elite | Context | Source |
|--------|------|-------|-------|---------|--------|
| Consumer social 6-mo user retention | ~25% | ~45% | ~60%+ | Free, ad-supported (Snap, Twitter, IG). Denominator = registered users | `what-is-good-retention.md` |
| Consumer transactional 6-mo user retention | ~30% | ~50% | ~70%+ | One-off purchases (Airbnb, Lyft, TurboTax). Denominator = users with 1+ transaction | `what-is-good-retention.md` |
| Consumer SaaS 6-mo user retention | ~40% | ~70% | ~80%+ | Monthly/yearly sub (Netflix, Spotify). Denominator = paid subscribers | `what-is-good-retention.md` |
| SMB/mid-market SaaS 6-mo user retention | ~60% | ~80% | ~90%+ | 100-1,000 employee companies (Asana, Slack, Atlassian) | `what-is-good-retention.md` |
| Enterprise SaaS 6-mo user retention | ~70% | ~90% | ~95%+ | 1,000+ employee companies (Salesforce, Workday, ADP) | `what-is-good-retention.md` |
