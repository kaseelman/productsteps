# How Miro Builds Product

> Company Playbook | Source: Lenny's Newsletter & Podcast

## Company Overview

Miro is a collaborative visual workspace used by over 50 million users, valued at $17.5 billion (one of the 10 most valuable U.S. startups at time of profiling). Its product organization of ~700 people (called AMPED) is notable for its structured approach to OKRs, multi-stage product reviews, codified product principles, and a "painted picture" vision process. Under CPO Varun Parmar, Miro balances operational rigor with creative energy, maintaining a strong emphasis on shipping customer value fast with high quality.

## Org Structure

- **AMPED cross-functional teams:** The product org is composed of Analytics, (Product) Marketing, Product, Engineering, and Design -- hence AMPED. Product Marketing is embedded from day one, not bolted on at launch.
- **Seven product streams:**
  1. Core product experience
  2. Enterprise
  3. Growth
  4. Core product foundation
  5. Infrastructure
  6. Platform
  7. Data
- **50+ PMs** within the ~700-person AMPED org.
